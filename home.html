My E-Resume

An e-resume (electronic resume) is a digital version of a traditional resume, designed to be viewed, shared, and submitted online. It is typically optimized for modern hiring processes, including Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) that scan resumes for keywords related to skills, experience, and education. This ensures that important information is easily readable not only by human recruiters but also by automated systems during online job applications.
